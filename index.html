ℹ️ About SakayTayo
The story behind this tool
🛵 For the Riders of Manolo Fortich
Know Your Fare.
Know Your Rights.
A free, no-account tool to protect passengers and support honest habal-habal drivers in Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.
🌱 Why this was made

With the implementation of the new Habal-Habal Flexible Fare Matrix under Joint Resolution No.1 s. 2026, many passengers — especially the elderly and those unfamiliar with technology — are unaware of the official fares. This leads to confusion, and sometimes overcharging.

SakayTayo was built to put the official fare matrix in everyone's pocket, with an easy map-based calculator so anyone can know exactly how much their ride should cost before they even mount the motorcycle.

No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Just a fair ride.

⚙️ How it works

The map uses OpenStreetMap data. When you drop a pickup and drop-off pin, the app measures the straight-line (aerial) distance between both points and references the official fare table to compute your fare instantly.

All calculations are stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. The Report feature generates a printable slip you can use when filing a complaint with the LGU or MFRTA.
